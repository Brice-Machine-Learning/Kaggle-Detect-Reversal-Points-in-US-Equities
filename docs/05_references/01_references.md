## Time Series Notes

- [Time Series Analysis and Its Applications](https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9783319524511) – A comprehensive textbook covering various aspects of time series analysis.
- [Forecasting: Principles and Practice](https://otexts.com/fpp3/) – An online textbook that provides practical guidance on forecasting methods and applications.
- [A Complete Guide to Time Series Analysis in Python](https://www.machinelearningplus.com/time-series/time-series-analysis-python/) – A detailed tutorial on performing time series analysis using Python.
